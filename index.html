Objectives

KoninLondon will try to tell the story about people living in London from a Cambodian woman's perspective. What do you see, hear, smell, feel ...

What Londoners do ? What are the differences between London and Phnom Penh (Where I was born and raised).

About me

I am Cambodian. I was born and raised in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. I have lived in Manchester, Hilversum (the Netherlands), and London for the last 4 years. I would like to share what I experience in London.
